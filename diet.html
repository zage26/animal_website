The Honey Badger

Diet

Honey badgers favor bee honey, and will often search for beehives to get it, which earns them
their name. (Honey badgers have even been known to chase away young lions and take their kills.)
They are also carnivorous and will eat:

  • Insects
  • Frogs
  • Tortoises
  • Rodents
  • Turtles
  • Lizards
  • Snakes
  • Eggs
  • Birds
